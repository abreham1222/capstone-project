ATLANTIS WORLD CUP THE GREATEST LUXURY EVENT IN HORSE RACING 2022

In 10 years, Atlantis World Cup has rapidly established as South Florida’s leading luxury lifestyle and sports event of the year. Nationally broadcast on TV and globally distributed, Atlantis World Cup attracts A-list celebrity attendees, influencers and avid fans to catch a glimpse of the sport’s most iconic event of the year.

Launched in 2012 as part of the race revolution movement to excite, motivate and modernize the sport of racing for a new wave of fans, the Atlantis World Cup provides lucrative opportunities for racing horses owners and trainers.
