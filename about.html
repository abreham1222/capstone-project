GOLF CLUB WORLD CUP THE GREATEST LUXURY EVENT IN GOLF CLUB 2022

In 10 years, Golf Club World Cup has rapidly established as South Florida’s leading luxury lifestyle and sports event of the year. Nationally broadcast on TV and globally distributed, Golf Club Cup attracts A-list celebrity attendees, influencers and avid fans to catch a glimpse of the sport’s most iconic event of the year.

Launched in 2012 as part of the golf revolution movement to excite, motivate and modernize the sport of golf for a new wave of fans, the Golf Club Cup provides lucrative opportunities for Golf Club owners and trainers.
